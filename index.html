ABOUT

The 2018 PyeongChang Winter Olympics was the 23rd Winter Olympics held from February 9 to February 25, 2018.
It is the first Winter Olympics held in Korea and the third Winter Olympics held in Asia.

  • OLYMPIC SPIRIT

    Athletes live their dreams on the world stage with the Olympic spirit. This is a competition that gives global influence and inspiration.

  • PEACE

    The Olympics are a celebration of peace and friendship. The world comes together to provide an opportunity to build understanding and friendship between nations through sport.

  • SPORT

    The Olympics are made up of a variety of events, with various sports elements reflected in the games, and top-level athletes compete.

  • INTERNATIONAL

    Nations from all over the world participate in the Olympics. Players from different cultures, languages and nationalities come together to play the game.
